Rubber Ducky Debugging

Yes, It's a real thing.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rubber_duck_debugging

The Ducky Process

  1. Get stuck on something.
  2. Get a rubber duck or equivalent.
  3. Assume the duck knows absolutely nothing about what you are doing...
  4. or anything about programming.
  5. Explain the problem out loud in extreme detail to the duck.
  6. You will often find out the problem yourself.

Benefits of Ducky Debugging?

  • Forces you to go through the thought process of your problem.
  • Makes you craft you question in a way an duck can understand it.
  • Requires you to put some serious effort into your question.
  • Helps you teach yourself.
  • Doesn't bother your coworkers.
