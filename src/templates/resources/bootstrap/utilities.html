Formula

Available Values

Properties

  • p - padding
  • m - margin

Sides (optional)

  • t|b|l|r - top, bottom, left or right
  • x|y - both sides or top and bottom
  • blank - applies to all sides

Size

  • 0 - 5
  • auto
