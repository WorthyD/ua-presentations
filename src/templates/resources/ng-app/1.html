Let's start building that app.

Open your terminal

CD into a non-git directory to start your work.

Create your app

ng new [name] --routing --style=scss --dry-run

Replace [name] with what you want to name your app.

  • Examples:
  • ng new taco-truck --routing --style=scss --dry-run
  • ng new blockbuster-reopening --routing --style=scss --dry-run

Verify your app is properly scaffolded in the dry run.

  • Do you see /src/app.component.scss
  • Do you see /src/app-routing-module.ts

Run the code without the --dry-run

You can press up on your arrow pad and just delete it.
